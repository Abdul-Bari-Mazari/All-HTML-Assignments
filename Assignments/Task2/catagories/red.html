Red (Lal) Tikka

Red tikka, also known as "Lal Tikka" in some regions, refers to a spicy and flavorful grilled or roasted chicken dish. It gets its name from the vibrant red color that is achieved through the use of spices and marinades. The red tikka chicken can be enjoyed as a main course dish alongside naan, rice, or a salad. Adjust the spice levels according to your preference. Enjoy your red tikka chicken!
